Evaluation

At the conclusion of the internship, mentors/managers complete a brief performance feedback survey. This feedback is a key input when assigning final grades.

End-of-Term Feedback Survey

Due Date: April 15, 2026

Survey Link: Complete the Feedback Survey

What the Survey Covers

The feedback survey asks you to evaluate your intern on:

  • Quality of work delivered
  • Communication and professionalism
  • Initiative and problem-solving
  • Reliability and follow-through
  • Growth and learning throughout the term
  • Overall performance

Sharing Feedback

  • Feedback is shared with the intern
  • We encourage you to discuss feedback verbally with your intern as part of a wrap-up conversation
  • This conversation is a valuable learning opportunity for the student

Wrap-Up Conversation Tips

Consider discussing:

  1. Highlights — What did they do well? What were you impressed by?
  2. Growth areas — Where could they improve? What skills should they develop?
  3. Career advice — What would help them succeed in product management?
  4. Next steps — Are there opportunities to continue working together?

Timeline

Milestone Date
Internship ends April 15, 2026
Feedback survey due April 15, 2026
Grades submitted April 22, 2026

Post-Internship (After April 15)

  • Complete the feedback survey by April 15
  • Have a wrap-up conversation with your intern
  • Optional: Discuss summer extension opportunities if interested
